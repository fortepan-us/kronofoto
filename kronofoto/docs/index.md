# Kronofoto

**Kronofoto** is an extremely user-friendly upload and display tool, which
arranges images along a simple but engaging timeline. Using Kronofoto, users
can fast forward toward the present or backward deeper into the past. The
code for Kronofoto can be used for all sorts of different collection types
beyond photographs.

Unlike archive-style digital repositories, which often mirror the priorities
of physical archives and divide collections by provenance, Kronofoto
prioritizes the experience of browsing to explore images of the past by
time, supported by robust metadata, tagging, and search features.

Kronofoto features a suite of tools to engage the public in visual history:

- **MyList** — customized lists of photos, viewable in grid or timeline
  view, saved for future reference, shared, or embedded.
- **Embed** — lets community institutions embed any view of a collection
  (a search, a `FotoAlbum`, an exhibit) into their own site via the
  `<fortepan-viewer>` custom element.
- **Exhibit** — curate a digital exhibit or story from cards/figures with a
  choice of text+image layouts.
- **Krono360** — an immersive visual geolocation tool that situates historic
  photographs in the modern landscape.
